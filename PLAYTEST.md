# Dadventure Playtest Checklist

Use this after starting the local server with `npm run serve` or `python -m http.server 8000`.

## Setup

- Start a fresh game with `Reset Progress` from the hub if needed.
- Enter a Dad name and choose an avatar.
- Confirm the hub shows six portals and six token slots.

## Worlds

- Motocross: try a bad timing setup and confirm the start gate stall happens.
- Motocross: use gas, steering, and trick/boost; confirm heat can slow/stall/explode the bike.
- Motocross: win and confirm the Gearheart Token unlocks.
- Creature League: walk around the map with buttons or WASD/arrow keys.
- Creature League: catch all six creatures; confirm the fifth failed catch auto-catches.
- Creature League: complete all three tournament rounds and confirm Teamheart unlocks.
- Jedi: complete asteroid run, saber defense, light-side choices, and duel.
- Guardian: collect five fragments, fight The Distance, and confirm Lightheart unlocks.
- Pixie: collect keys, cross the cove, unlock the cage, win the duel, and confirm Pixieheart unlocks.
- Fishing: set the hook on a strike, manage tension/stamina, land the swordfish, and confirm Tideheart unlocks.

## Final Door

- Confirm the final door is locked before all six tokens are earned.
- Confirm it unlocks after all six tokens are earned.
- Confirm the final six-token gallery displays.

## Notes To Record

- Anything confusing.
- Any level that feels too easy.
- Any level that feels too hard.
- Any line that feels too cheesy, not funny enough, or emotionally off.
- Any mobile button that is hard to press.
